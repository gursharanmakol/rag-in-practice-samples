# TechNova Warranty Terms

TechNova products are covered by a limited warranty from the date of original purchase. This warranty applies to products purchased from TechNova directly or through authorized retailers.

## Coverage Period

All TechNova audio products carry a 1-year limited warranty. The WH-1000 Premium Headphones and WH-500 Sport Headphones are covered for 12 months from the date of purchase. The TechNova SB-300 Soundbar is covered for 18 months. Replacement accessories (ear cushions, cables, carrying cases) are covered for 90 days.

## What Is Covered

This warranty covers defects in materials and workmanship under normal use. Covered issues include battery failure to hold a charge within the warranty period, hardware malfunctions not caused by physical damage, Bluetooth connectivity failures traced to a hardware defect, and audio quality degradation not caused by external factors.

## What Is Not Covered

This warranty does not cover damage resulting from misuse, accidents, unauthorized modifications, or normal wear and tear. Specifically excluded are water damage beyond the product's rated IP protection level, physical damage from drops or impacts, damage caused by use with unauthorized third-party accessories, and cosmetic damage such as scratches or dents that do not affect functionality.

## How to File a Warranty Claim

Contact TechNova Support at support@technova.com with your proof of purchase, product serial number, and a description of the issue. Our support team will troubleshoot the issue remotely when possible. If the product requires repair or replacement, TechNova will provide shipping instructions and a prepaid return label.

## Repair and Replacement

TechNova reserves the right to repair or replace defective products at its discretion. Replacement products may be new or refurbished with equivalent functionality. Repaired or replaced products are covered for the remainder of the original warranty period or 90 days, whichever is longer.

## Limitations

This warranty is the sole warranty provided by TechNova. TechNova's liability under this warranty is limited to repair, replacement, or refund of the purchase price. TechNova is not responsible for incidental or consequential damages arising from the use of its products.

For returns within the 15-day return window, see our Return Policy.
