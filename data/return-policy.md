# TechNova Return Policy

TechNova offers a 15-day return window on all products purchased directly from TechNova or through authorized retailers. The return period begins on the date of delivery, not the date of purchase.

## Eligibility

To be eligible for a return, the product must be in its original packaging with all included accessories, cables, and documentation. Products must show no signs of physical damage beyond normal unboxing. Proof of purchase is required for all returns — this can be the original receipt, order confirmation email, or shipping confirmation.

## How to Initiate a Return

Contact TechNova Support at support@technova.com or call 1-800-555-0199 to request a return authorization number. Include your order number and the reason for the return. TechNova will provide a prepaid shipping label for products purchased directly from our store. Returns through authorized retailers follow that retailer's return shipping process.

## Refund Processing

Once we receive and inspect the returned product, refunds are processed within 5 business days. Refunds are issued to the original payment method. Shipping costs are non-refundable unless the return is due to a TechNova error or a defective product.

## Exceptions

The following items are not eligible for return: products with physical damage caused by misuse, products missing original packaging or accessories, and products purchased more than 15 days before the return request. Refurbished products purchased through the TechNova Outlet have a 7-day return window instead of the standard 15 days.

For warranty claims beyond the return window, see our Warranty Terms.
